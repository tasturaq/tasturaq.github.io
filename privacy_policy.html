Application Privacy Policy

Tabot values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. Here are the key points of our privacy policy:

  • Allow this application to run when you are not present: https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.scriptapp
  • View and manage your forms in Google Drive: https://www.googleapis.com/auth/forms
  • Connect to an external service: https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.external_request
  • Display and run third-party web content in prompts and sidebars inside Google applications: https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.container.ui

Tabot does not collect or store any personal data from your Google Forms or Telegram chats. All data is securely transmitted and processed solely to provide the integration functionality. We do not share your data with third parties under any circumstances. You retain full control over the integration and can disable it at any time.
